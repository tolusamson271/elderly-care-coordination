# Elderly Care Coordination

A comprehensive senior services platform with caregiver scheduling, health monitoring, family communication, and emergency response for aging populations.

## Key Features

• **Senior & Caregiver Registration** - Register seniors and caregivers with profiles and qualifications
• **Appointment Scheduling** - Schedule and manage care appointments between seniors and caregivers  
• **Health Monitoring** - Record vital signs, medications, and health notes
• **Emergency Response** - Create and respond to emergency alerts with priority levels
• **Family Communication** - Coordinate care among family members and emergency contacts
